The Story

From quantum problems
to money problems.

Dr. Sofina Johari

In 2016 I was a fresh physics graduate who could model battery materials at the atomic level — yet I struggled to navigate my own personal finances. Nobody had taught me. That frustration became my calling.

I started my journey with UOB Kay Hian since 2020 and have since been mastering the craft, earned my licenses, and completed my PhD along the way. Today I apply the same discipline I used in the lab to one question: how do we make your money work, honestly and optimally?

As a wealth advisor here, I have access to 8+ insurance and takaful providers, hundreds of unit trust options across different fund managers, and trustee companies. My recommendations answer to your needs as my client, not to any single product house. Whether you prefer conventional or Shariah-compliant solutions, my door is open to every Malaysian.
