My Story

From quantum problems
to money problems.

Dr. Sofina Johari

In 2016, I was a fresh physics graduate who could model battery materials at the atomic level, yet I couldn't manage my own personal finances. No one had ever taught me. That frustration became my calling.

I started my journey with UOB Kay Hian in 2020. Since then, I've earned my licenses, completed my PhD, and mastered the craft of personal finance. Today, I bring the same discipline I once used in the lab to a single question: how do we make your money work honestly and optimally?

As a wealth advisor in UOB Kay Hian, I have access to over eight insurance and takaful providers, hundreds of unit trust options across different fund managers, and multiple trustee companies. My recommendations answer to your needs as my client, not to any single product house. Whether you prefer conventional or Shariah-compliant solutions, my door is open to every Malaysian.
