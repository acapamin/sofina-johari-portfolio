The Story

From quantum problems
to money problems.

In 2016 I was a fresh physics graduate who could model battery materials at the atomic level — yet I struggled to navigate my own personal finances. Nobody had taught me. That frustration became my calling.

I spent four years at UOB Kay Hian Wealth Advisors learning the craft, earned my licenses, and completed my PhD along the way. Today I apply the same discipline I used in the lab to one question: how do we make your money work, honestly and optimally?

As an independent advisor with access to 8+ takaful and insurance providers and hundreds of unit trust options, my recommendations answer to you — not to any single product house.
